Biography

Yayati Naresh Palai is a experimental chemist hailing from the state of Odisha, located in the estern coast India. He finished his Ph.D. in 2022 at the Graduate School of Chemical Sciences and Engineering, Hokkaido University, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Atsushi Fukuoka and mentorhip of Dr. Abhijit Shrotri. His research involves finding new sustainable and energy efficient pathways for making chemicals with four cabon backbone. Currently, he is employed as postdoctoral research fellow at Institute for Catalysis, with Prof. Dr. Atsushi Fukuoka's group.
